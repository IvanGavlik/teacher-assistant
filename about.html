Our story

Built by teachers, for teachers

We started Teacher Assistant because we spent too many Sunday evenings preparing vocabulary sheets instead of enjoying life. We knew there had to be a better way.

2023 Founded
5,000+ Active teachers
6+ Languages supported

Language learning changes lives. Teachers deserve tools that match that importance.

We saw the problem firsthand. Language teachers were spending four to six hours every week creating materials from scratch — writing vocabulary lists, crafting grammar exercises, finding appropriate reading texts. Time that could have been spent with students, with family, or simply resting.

So we built Teacher Assistant. A tool that generates complete, CEFR-aligned lesson materials in seconds. Vocabulary builders, grammar explanations, reading passages, and exercises — all tailored to the exact level and language you're teaching.

We're a small team with a focused mission: give language teachers back their time without ever compromising on the quality their students deserve.

What drives us

Our values

Teachers first

Every feature starts with a real classroom need. We talk to teachers constantly and only build what genuinely saves time or improves outcomes.

Quality over quantity

We'd rather generate one perfect exercise than ten mediocre ones. Accuracy, CEFR alignment, and pedagogical soundness are non-negotiable.

Keep it simple

Teachers are busy. The tool should feel obvious the first time you use it. No onboarding videos, no 30-page manuals — just describe what you need and get it.
