About Teacher Assistant

Teacher Assistant generates vocabulary lists, grammar explanations, reading passages, and exercises — all CEFR-aligned — for language teachers in seconds. We need someone who deeply understands language teaching to make sure every piece of content is pedagogically sound and genuinely useful in the classroom.

What we're looking for

  • Background in language teaching, linguistics, or curriculum design
  • Solid understanding of the CEFR framework (A1–C2)
  • Ability to evaluate AI-generated content for accuracy and pedagogical quality
  • Clear communicator who can translate classroom experience into product feedback
  • Bonus: experience with multiple target languages

How to apply

Send an email to info@wortkraftmaster.org with the subject line Pedagogy — [Your name]. Tell us about your teaching background and what good language learning materials look like to you.
